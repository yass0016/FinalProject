Adding a device

To add a device, click the button in the bottom right corner. This is known as a FAB, or a Floating Action Button.

In the dialog that pops up, you enter the name and model of the device, along with selecting the kind of device it is; a Refrigerator or Coffee Maker.

Device Info

Clicking on a device you have added shows a dialog where you can display info about your device. From here you can delete the device or configure it.
